Summary

I am Tomohiro MOTODA, a Ph.D. course student from Osaka, Japan. I enrolled in Osaka University in 2014 and proceeded to Graduate school at Osaka University in 2018. My research target is robotic manipulation, and I aim to develop a new bin-picking system considering safety and simplicity. I have computer language skills, including C, C++, Python, ROS programming (intermediate), etc.
